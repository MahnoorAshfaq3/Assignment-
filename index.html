Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Method:

Step 1:
Preheat oven to 180oC.
Step 2:
Mix dry ingredients separately.
Step 3:
Cream butter and sugars, add egg.
Step 4:
Fold in chocolate chips & bake 10-12 min.
